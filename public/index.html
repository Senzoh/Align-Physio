REQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

Why should I visit a physiotherapist?

+

You should wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows for easy movement and access to the area being treated. If you're attending for a lower body issue, wearing shorts or loose-fitting pants that can be rolled up is ideal. For upper body concerns, a sleeveless top or a loose T-shirt works best. If your session involves movement assessments, athletic wear and trainers are recommended. Avoid jeans, tight clothing, or restrictive footwear, as they may limit movement and make assessment or treatment more difficult.
