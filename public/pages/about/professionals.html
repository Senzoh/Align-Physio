From Frustration to Foundation

Align Physio was founded after our clinical director saw a loved one wait over 12 weeks for basic physiotherapy after surgery. By the time help came, scar tissue had already formed, and recovery became more painful and drawn-out than it needed to be.

That moment sparked a vision — to create a physiotherapy service that puts patients first. We started small, with home visits and one portable treatment bed. Today, we help hundreds recover faster, with no waitlists and expert advice from day one.

We believe healing should be proactive, not reactive — and it should never depend on how long you're willing to wait.
