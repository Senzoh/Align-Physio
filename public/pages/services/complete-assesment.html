FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

What is included in the Complete Physical Assessment?

+

This 60-minute session includes a full-body evaluation of your mobility, posture, strength, and overall wellness. After the assessment, your physiotherapist will recommend and carry out two tailored treatments suited to your needs.

What treatments are offered after the assessment?

+

Based on your results and preferences, the therapist will select and deliver two treatments from the following: acupuncture, massage therapy, PNF stretching, or cupping therapy.

Who is this session suitable for?

+

The assessment is ideal for anyone looking to understand their physical health, prevent injuries, or target specific pain or dysfunction. It’s especially useful for first-time clients or those unsure which treatment they need.

Do I need to prepare anything before the session?

+

Please wear comfortable, flexible clothing suitable for movement and stretching. If you have any recent scans, medical notes, or past injuries, bring them along—they help your therapist tailor the session to you.
