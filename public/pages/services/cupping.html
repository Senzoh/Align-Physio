FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

What is cupping therapy and how does it work?

+

Cupping therapy uses suction to lift the skin and underlying tissues, improving blood flow, loosening tight muscles, and promoting healing. It’s often used for pain relief, inflammation, and tension release.

Does cupping leave marks on the skin?

+

Yes, cupping often leaves circular marks that resemble bruises. These are not painful and usually fade within a few days to a week. The discoloration is a sign of improved blood flow and toxin release.

Is cupping therapy painful?

+

Cupping is generally not painful. You may feel a tight or pulling sensation where the cups are placed. Most clients find it relaxing, and discomfort is minimal and brief.

What should I do before and after cupping therapy?

+

Before your session, eat lightly and stay hydrated. After cupping, avoid strenuous exercise, hot showers, or exposure to cold for 24 hours. Drink plenty of water to support detoxification.
