FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

What are the benefits of massage therapy?

+

Massage therapy can relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, reduce stress, enhance sleep, boost immunity, and support recovery from injury. It also promotes relaxation and overall well-being.

What should I expect during a massage session?

+

Your therapist will begin with a brief consultation to understand your needs. You'll be covered with a towel or sheet, and only the area being worked on will be exposed. Pressure can be adjusted to your comfort.

Is massage therapy painful?

+

Massage should not be painful. Some deep tissue techniques may cause mild discomfort, but always communicate with your therapist. The goal is relief—not pain. Adjustments are always possible.

How often should I get a massage?

+

Frequency depends on your goals. For general wellness, once a month may be enough. For stress, chronic tension, or injury recovery, weekly or bi-weekly sessions may be more effective.
