FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

What is included in the post-surgery rehab plan?

+

The program includes a personalised rehabilitation plan tailored to your surgery, regular check-ins with a physiotherapist, progress tracking, and plan adjustments as you heal. Our focus is on safe, steady recovery and improved outcomes.

Who is this program for?

+

This program is designed for individuals recovering from orthopedic or soft tissue surgeries, such as knee, hip, shoulder, or spinal procedures. It’s perfect if you need more hands-on guidance than the NHS currently offers.

How does this differ from standard NHS rehab?

+

Unlike NHS care, our plan offers consistent support without long wait times. You’ll have more frequent sessions, faster progression, and a higher degree of personalisation—ensuring your rehab evolves as your body heals.

What if I have pain or setbacks during recovery?

+

Your therapist will monitor your progress closely and adapt your plan if pain or complications arise. We take a responsive, supportive approach to help you recover safely and confidently—even if things don’t go perfectly.
