FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - FAQ

Everything you need to know, answered.

What is included in a virtual consultation?

+

A 30-minute video call with a certified physiotherapist where you’ll receive personalised advice on pain, injury, rehab, or mobility concerns. You’ll also get a written follow-up with your next steps and recommendations.

What can I use a virtual consultation for?

+

It’s ideal for pain assessments, rehab advice, post-treatment guidance, or general mobility and wellness check-ins. Whether you're recovering from injury or seeking expert input, it's a convenient way to get answers.

Do I need any special equipment?

+

No specialist equipment is required. Just a stable internet connection, a smartphone or computer with a camera, and a quiet, well-lit space where you can move freely for any assessments.

Is a virtual session as effective as in-person?

+

While hands-on treatment isn’t possible, virtual consultations are highly effective for advice, rehab planning, pain education, and mobility guidance. Many clients find them just as helpful, especially for early intervention and follow-up.
